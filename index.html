Leap

Unreal Engine April Game Jam Entry

Leap is a game I made for an online competition created by Unreal Engine developers. The theme of the Jam was “Sprint Into the Future” and the challange was to make a video game in 3 days. My idea was to represent through the game’s mechanics one of the risks of traveling into the future, like not knowing if you will be safe in it. You play as a cube that has the power to advance in time, the power is called Leap and it allows you to heal yourself and travel a few seconds ahead of time.

You can download the game in this sample from itch.io.

Dawn of Missile Command

Orusys Unity 3D Test

This mini game is based on the original Missile Command. The idea is that you need to defend your city from incoming missiles. To do so, you are given three missile towers from which you can shoot missiles that explode where you want to. But beware, as you shoot, the towers will become hot and if you reach it’s maximum temperature they will enter in a cooldown period in which you cannot shoot. You lose when all the cities are destroyed. If a missile tower is destroyed it will be respawned the next round… if you survive. Have fun.

You can download the game and the unity project in this sample from itch.io.
